Our Business Journey

2015

The Beginning of ShopoxHome

Founded by passionate event enthusiasts, ShopoxHome Photo Booth Rentals started with a single photo booth unit and a dream. Our founders recognized the growing demand for interactive entertainment at events and set out to create a service that combined quality, fun, and professionalism. The first year saw 45 successful events and laid the foundation for future growth.

2016

Expanding Our Equipment Fleet

Rapidly growing demand led to the acquisition of additional photo booth units and upgraded equipment. We invested in professional-grade cameras and lighting systems to ensure superior photo quality. By year-end, we had served over 100 events and expanded our service area to include neighboring cities.

2017

Corporate Partnerships Established

A pivotal year marked by partnerships with major corporations and event planning companies. We became the preferred photo booth vendor for several Fortune 500 companies' regional events. Our growing reputation for reliability and quality attracted high-profile clients seeking professional event services.

2018

Technology Innovation Launch

Embracing technological advancement, we launched our digital sharing platform and mobile-friendly features. Guests could now instantly share photos on social media directly from the booth. This innovation dramatically increased engagement and positioned us as industry leaders in photo booth technology.

2019

Regional Expansion Achieved

Our service area expanded to cover 25 cities across the region. A second operational hub was established to better serve our growing client base. We celebrated our 500th event milestone and received recognition from local business associations for excellence in service.

2020

Adapting to New Challenges

During unprecedented times, we pivoted to offer contactless photo booth options and virtual event solutions. Safety protocols were implemented across all operations. Despite challenges, we continued serving clients with modified services, demonstrating our commitment to customer needs in any circumstance.

2021

Strong Recovery & Growth

As events returned in full swing, we experienced record-breaking demand. Our fleet expanded to 15 units, and we welcomed new team members to meet growing needs. We launched enhanced packages for weddings and corporate events, setting new standards in the photo booth industry.

2022

Service Excellence Recognition

Awarded "Best Photo Booth Service" by regional event industry publications. We achieved our goal of serving 50 cities and celebrated over 750 successful events. Our customer satisfaction ratings reached an all-time high, reflecting our unwavering commitment to exceptional service.

2023

Innovation & Expansion

Launched our next-generation photo booth technology featuring AI-powered features and enhanced customization options. Expanded our prop collection to over 500 items and introduced themed packages for specialized events. Our team grew to include dedicated designers and event specialists.

2024

Continuing the Journey

Today, ShopoxHome Photo Booth Rentals continues to innovate and grow. We've expanded our service offerings to include video booths, 360-degree photo experiences, and green screen technology. Our mission remains unchanged: creating memorable experiences that bring joy to every event we serve.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a photo booth?
We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance, especially for weekend events during peak seasons. However, we occasionally have last-minute availability, so don't hesitate to contact us even if your event is coming up soon. Our team will do their best to accommodate your needs.
What is included in a standard photo booth rental?
Our standard package includes professional photo booth equipment, high-speed printing, unlimited photo sessions, a variety of props, custom photo templates, digital copies of all photos, and a professional attendant to manage the booth throughout your event.
How much space is needed for the photo booth setup?
We typically require a minimum space of 8x8 feet for our standard booth setup, with access to power outlets. For larger events or custom configurations, we may need additional space. We'll discuss specific requirements during the booking process to ensure your venue can accommodate the setup.
Can the photo booth be set up outdoors?
Yes! We offer outdoor photo booth services with weather-resistant equipment. We do require a flat, covered area to protect the equipment from direct sunlight, rain, or wind. Our team will assess the location during planning to ensure optimal setup conditions.
Do you provide props with the photo booth?
Absolutely! We bring an extensive collection of fun props including hats, glasses, boas, signs, and themed accessories. Our prop collection is regularly updated and can be customized to match your event theme. Special themed props are available upon request.
How long does it take to set up the photo booth?
Setup typically takes 60-90 minutes depending on the configuration and venue. We always arrive well before your event start time to ensure everything is ready. Our team handles all setup and breakdown, so you can focus on enjoying your event.
Can guests share photos on social media?
Yes! Our photo booths feature instant social media sharing capabilities. Guests can email or text their photos directly from the booth and share to their favorite social platforms. We also provide a digital gallery after the event for easy access to all photos.
What areas do you serve?
ShopoxHome Photo Booth Rentals currently serves over 48 cities across our region. We're continuously expanding our service area. Contact us with your event location, and we'll confirm availability. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our standard service zone.
